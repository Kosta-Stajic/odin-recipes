Lasagna

Description

To make a good lasagna we will focus on getting the best ingredients and techniques. It takes a little work but it's worth it in the long run!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce(Making it from scratch is worth it if you have the time)
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarela cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water. Season well
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook your lasagna noodles per package instruction
  3. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  4. Assemble the dish by spreading the meat sauce at the bottom, followed by lasagna noodles lengthwise. Spread your cheese mixture and more meat sauce. Repeat the process until you top it up.
  5. Bake in preheated over for 50 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving
