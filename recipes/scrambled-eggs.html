Scrambled Eggs

Description

Take 3 eggs and crack them into a small size sauce pan. Mix gently until everything is combined together. Add 2-3 nobs of butter.

Cook on low heat and continuously stir until the eggs reach your desired texture. Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve with the most amazing brioche toast.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Brioche

Steps

  1. Crack eggs
  2. Mix you idiot
  3. Add buttah!
  4. Put on low heat
  5. Stir until you're happy with the texture
  6. Add spices and serve with toast
